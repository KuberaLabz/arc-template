The Studio

Twelve years.
One principle.

ARC was founded in 2012 by Nadia Wren and James Osei with one principle: that the best buildings are the ones you can't imagine not existing. The ones that feel inevitable in retrospect.

We're a studio of twelve — architects, interior designers, landscape specialists, and project managers — working across the US and UK. We work slowly and deliberately, and we take very few projects each year.
